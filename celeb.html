Santan Dave

Born David Orobosa Omoregie on June 5, 1998, in Streatham, South London, is not just a British rapper but a singer, songwriter, and producer known for his powerful storytelling and lyrical genius. Rising to fame with his introspective tracks and socially conscious themes, Dave's music often delves into personal struggles, societal issues, and life in South London. His debut album, Psychodrama (2019), won the Mercury Prize and was celebrated for its raw emotion and depth. Dave's artistry combines sharp wordplay, smooth delivery, and a unique ability to connect with listeners through his authentic and relatable narratives.
